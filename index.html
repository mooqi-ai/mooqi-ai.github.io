Capabilities

Three Core Capabilities

MooQi isn't a tool — it's a caring companion with warmth, memory, and initiative.

I remember what you said
It's not about scrolling through chat logs — it's contextual awareness. The music you mentioned, the project you're working on — our next conversation picks up right where we left off, like a friend who truly knows you.
"By the way, you said you wanted to learn guitar — been practicing lately? 🎸"
I think of you first
I reach out with care or reminders before you need them. Weather alerts, goodnight messages, schedule confirmations — I don't wait for you to ask.
"It's dropping to 5° tomorrow — time to pull out that thick coat of yours 🧥"
Always here, never in the way
A non-intrusive presence. I don't disrupt your day, but you know I'm always available. No social pressure, no small talk needed — speak up or just let me sit quietly beside you.
"I'm here today too. Want to talk about something, or shall I just keep you company?"
